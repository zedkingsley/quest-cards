## The Family Dynamic

### Everyone Participates

- **Kids** do quests assigned by parents
- **Kids** can challenge parents to quests (role reversal!)
- **Parents** can do quests and experiments too
- **Everyone** can see what others are working on (visibility = accountability)

### Parents as Coaches

Parents aren't just task-assigners. They're **curators of growth**:
- Browse quest packs to find what's right for each kid
- Save quests to a **backlog** for later ("Wyatt isn't ready for this yet")
- Think about each child's unique journey
- Celebrate growth moments, not just completion

---

## The Rewards Philosophy

**Points** = Universal currency earned by growth
**Rewards** = Promises family members make to each other

- Kids earn points, redeem from parents' reward shops
- Parents earn points too, redeem from each other
- It's a family economy of mutual investment
